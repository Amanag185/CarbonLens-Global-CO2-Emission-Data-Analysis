# CarbonLens: Global CO₂ Emission Data Analysis

CarbonLens is a data analytics and sustainability intelligence project focused on uncovering global carbon emission patterns through large-scale data analysis, visualization, and environmental impact assessment.

The project leverages Python, SQL, NumPy, Pandas, Tableau, and Power BI to transform raw climate datasets into actionable insights, enabling a deeper understanding of emission trends, population growth impacts, industrialization effects, and climate change indicators. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

# Executive Summary

Climate change is one of the most pressing global challenges of the 21st century, with carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions being a primary contributor to rising global temperatures.

This project analyzes historical global CO₂ emission datasets to:

- Identify long-term emission trends
- Examine the relationship between population growth and emissions
- Analyze country-level emission contributions
- Study the impact of industrialization and economic development
- Investigate climate indicators such as temperature rise
- Generate sustainability-focused recommendations

Through data-driven analysis, CarbonLens provides insights into how human activities influence environmental outcomes and highlights opportunities for sustainable development. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Population vs CO₂ Emissions

- Industrial production scales with population growth.
